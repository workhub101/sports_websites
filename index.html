Baseball Overview

Baseball is a bat and ball game that is played between two teams of 9 players each, that take turns playing offence and defence. Where winner of the game is decided by which team has the most runs after 9 innings. The innings are split into top half and bottom half where each team has a chance to bat. To score a run one player needs to cross all 4 bases, which are labled first, second, third and home.
